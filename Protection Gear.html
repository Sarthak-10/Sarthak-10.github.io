Protective Gear

Personal Protective Equipments (PPEs) are protective gears designed to safeguard the health of hospital staff workers by minimising the exposure to any biological agent.These protective gears are specially for the use by Covid patients,doctors,ambulance drivers and other medical Covid related staff.

Protective gears and general advisory for Common Public:-

The PPE kits which comprise of the invaluble protective items,are of great use to the medical staff,but the general public also needs to wear some protective equipment depending on their daily routines and professions.These include masks for all,gloves and face shield for those interacting with a lot of people,etc.The correct way to wear these and their importance have been described below...
